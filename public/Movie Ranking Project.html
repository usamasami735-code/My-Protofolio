Best Movies of According to Usama

My top 3 Movies of all time

DOOM 2005

Doom is a Science fiction film featuring a group of Marines who must fight their way through an alien-infested base to stop a powerful demon from escaping.

Underworld 2003

Underworld is a dark fantasy action film that follows the secret war between vampires and werewolves, focusing on a vampire named Selene who becomes entangled in the conflict.

BLADE 2002

Blade is a superhero film that follows the story of Blade, a half-vampire with superhuman abilities who hunts vampires.
